Stress-Reducing Techniques Instructions:

DEEP BREATHING EXERCISES

  1. Find a comfortable position: Sit or lie down in a quiet space.
  2. Close your eyes: Focus on your breath.
  3. Inhale slowly: Breathe in through your nose for 4-6 seconds.
  4. Hold your breath: Hold for 2-4 seconds
  5. Exhale slowly: Breathe out through your mouth for 4-6 seconds.
  6. Repeat:Continue for 5-10 minutes.

MINDFULNESS MEDITATION

  1. Find a quiet space: Sit comfortably with your back straight.
  2. Close your eyes: Focus on your breath or a mantra.
  3. Focus on your breath: Pay attention to the sensation of breath moving in and out of your body.
  4. When your mind wanders: Gently bring your attention back to your breath.
  5. Start small: Begin with 5-10 minutes sessions and increase as you become more comfortable.

PROGRESSIVE MUSCLE RELAXATION

  1. Find a comfortable position: Sit or lie down.
  2. Tense a muscle group: Start with your toes.
  3. Hold the tension: Hold for 5-10 seconds.
  4. Release the tension: Relax your toes.
  5. Move up your body Tense and relax each muscle group (feet, calves, thighs, arms, hands, neck, and head.
  6. Repeat: Continue for 10-15 minutes.

JOURNALLING

  1. Chose a journal: Select a notebook or a digital journal.
  2. Set aside time: Schedule time each day to journal.
  3. Write freely: Write about your thoughts, feelings, and experieces.
  4. Reflect on your day: Reflect on what you're grateful for and what you're looking forward to.
  5. Keep it private: Don't worry about grammar or spelling; this is for your eyes only.

GROUNDING TECHNIQUES

  1. Notice your surroundings: Pay attention to your five senses.
  2. See: Notice the sights around you.
  3. Hear: Notice the sounds around you.
  4. Smell: Notice any scents or smells.
  5. Taste: Notice any tastes or sensations in your mouth.
  6. Touch: Notice the sensation of your feet on the ground or the chair beneath you.

VISUALIZATION

  1. Find a quiet space: Sit or lie down comfortably.
  2. Close your eyes: Imagine yourself in a peaceful place
  3. Use all senses: Visualize sights, sounds, smell, tastes, and sensation.
  4. Create a scenario: Imagine yourself achieving a goal or feeling calm.
  5. Stay for a while: Spend 5-10 minutes in your peaceful place.

YOGA AND STRETCHING

  1. Find a quiet space: Roll out on a yoga mat or find a comfortable spot.
  2. Start with gentle stretches: Neck, shoulders,chest, and back.
  3. Move slowly: Focus on your breath and body.
  4. Listen to your body: Stop if you experience discomfort.
  5. End with relaxation: Deep breathing or meditation.

GRATITUDE PRACTICE

  1. Set aside time: Daily or weekly.
  2. Write down three things: You're grateful for.
  3. Explain why: Reflect on the positive impact.
  4. Share with someone: Optional but can enhance benefits.
  5. Reflect on gratitude: Notice how it shifts your perspective.

SELF-COMPASSION

  1. Practice self-kindness: Treat yourself with understanding.
  2. Acknowledge emotions: Recognize and validate feelings.
  3. Offer reassurance: Be gentle and supportive.
  4. Practice mindfulness: Stay present and aware.
  5. Develop a growth mindset: View challenges as opportunities.

NATURE CONNECTION

  1. Spend time outdoors: Walk, hike, or simply sit.
  2. Notice nature: Observe sights, sounds, and smells.
  3. Leave technology behind: Focus on the present.
  4. Engage your senses: Touch, smell, see, hear, and feel.
  5. Reflect on benefits: Notice how nature impacts your mood.

Mindfulness Techniques

  2. Mindful eating: Eat slowly, savour flavors, textures.
  3. Loving-kindness meditation: Focus on sending kindness to yourself and others.
  4. Grain of sand meditation: Focus on small objects, notice details.
  5. Sensory exploration: Focus on one sense(e.g, hearing, sight.)

Relaxation strategies

  2. Deep breathing exercises: Slow deep breath, calm the mind
  3. Visualization:Imagine peaceful scenes, engage senses
  4. Listening to music: Choose calming music focus on the sounds.
  5. Aromatherapy:Use essential oils, notice scents.
